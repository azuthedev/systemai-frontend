1 Flagship workflow

From WhatsApp messages to clean CRM records in seconds.

One of our clients, a fast‑growing real‑estate team, used to forward every lead and property update to virtual assistants, who then copied details into Zoho CRM and Google Sheets. Each task took 4–5 minutes and happened dozens of times a day. We built a WhatsApp assistant that ingests messages and voice notes, figures out whether it is a lead or a property update, and pushes structured data straight into their systems in 10–20 seconds.

The same pattern now underpins how we help other ops‑heavy teams handle chat‑ and email‑driven work without adding headcount.
